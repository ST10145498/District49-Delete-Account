How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to delete your account from the District49 app, please follow the steps below:

📱 In-App Account Deletion Process:

  • Open the District49 app.
  • Navigate to the Contact Us page.
  • Fill in the form with your registered account details.
  • In your message, clearly request: “Please delete my account.”
  • The admin team will review and permanently remove your account and associated data.

📧 Alternative: Email Request

If you are unable to access the app, you can send a deletion request via email to: [KyleGovender@gmail.com]

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • The email/phone number linked to your account
  • A clear statement requesting account deletion

🗑️ What Happens After Deletion?

  • Your account will be permanently removed.
  • This action is irreversible.
